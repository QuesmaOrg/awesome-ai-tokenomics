# Research & Tools

An annotated bibliography of tools, papers, benchmarks, and datasets relevant to AI tokenomics, organized by area, each entry dated and sourced. Every entry cites a primary source and a `verified_on` date; see [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md) for the entry format.

- [Measure](measure.md): benchmarks, cache accounting, cost anatomy, metering, and transcript-analysis tools and research.
- [Monitor](monitor.md): observability, tracing, dashboards, and telemetry for LLM/agent spend.
- [Optimize](optimize.md): caching, context engineering, routing, local models, and other cost-reduction tools and research.
- [Govern](govern.md): budgets, spend management, chargeback, policy enforcement, and energy/carbon accounting.
- [Understand](understand.md): market sizing, model economics, pricing models, and buyer-incentive research.

*Generated from the research atlas via an export pipeline; per-entry dates live in [manifest.json](manifest.json).*
